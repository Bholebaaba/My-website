2.Inserting images

Align attribute is used to set the alignment of an image relative to the text around it . The different values of align attribute are LEFT ,RIGHT and CENTER . To vertically align or wrap the text around image , we can assign one of the given values - TOP,BOTTOM and MIDDLE to align attribute .
When we display both the text and image together, the text is wrapped around the image . Sometimes the space between the text and image is too less. Therefore, to increase the horizontal and vertical space, we use HSPACE and VSPACE attribute of the img tag respectively .
